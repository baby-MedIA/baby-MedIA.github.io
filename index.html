ABOUT US

Biomedical Engineering & Imaging Sciences

We are proud members of King's College London, where we embrace our role within the broader university community. We actively engage with fellow researchers, students, and experts across disciplines, promoting interdisciplinary exchange.

Perinatal Imaging and Health

We are driven by a desire to develop medical image analysis tools to further our understanding of the mechanisms behind fetal, neonatal and children development.

Software Tools at our Github Page

We value open source code and actively contribute to the global scientific community by sharing our advancements, and fostering a culture of transparency and collaboration.

Check out our larger group, MeTrICS Lab

We believe that pooling our collective knowledge and skills fosters innovation and accelerates progress in the rapidly evolving field of medical image analysis.
