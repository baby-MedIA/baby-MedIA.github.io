Welcome to Baby-MedIA@KCL

We are a group of researchers based at King's College London,
developing image analysis tools to study perinatal development.

Our interests include:

  • Early Life Brain Development
  • Motion Corrected Fetal MRI
  • Image Analysis for Diffusion, Functional and Quantitative MRI
  • Fetal Ultrasound
  • Deep Learning Image Segmentation and Registration
  • Interpretable Machine Learning
